# BP06 - Direct Communication

Source diagram: `1.WorkflowDiagrams /BP06-DirectCommunication.bpmn`

## Workflow Description

This workflow sets up a direct communication channel between the customer and provider through WhatsApp. The system creates the channel, sends a welcome message to the customer, and forwards messages between the provider and customer as they communicate through the channel.

## Intention

The intention is to establish and maintain direct customer-provider communication while the system brokers channel setup, welcome messaging, and bidirectional message forwarding.
